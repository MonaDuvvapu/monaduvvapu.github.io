Optimizing Gap Trends and Market Strategy ( Code)

    Made with:

Domains: Web Scraping, Case Aanlysis, Competitive Strategy

  • Challenges: Forecasting fashion trends across design aspects like color, fabric, pattern and analyzing if the data-driven approach worked equally for Gap's three brands (Gap, Old Navy, Banana Republic).
  • Data sources identified: In-house data (sales, reviews, website traffic) and external data (competitors, Google trends, e-commerce best sellers, social media).
  • External data analysis: Competitor analysis (scraping Zara's jeans offerings), e-commerce ranking prediction model, and social media/review analysis.
  • Limitations: Small sample sizes for scraping competitors and e-commerce rankings across all product categories.
  • Recommendations: Balanced approach combining data-driven analysis with creative directors' intuition, especially for premium brands like Banana Republic.
